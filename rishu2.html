Romance across the globe!

Romance isn't for everyone, but the sign of a good romantic movie is if it can move even the most steely hearted grump in some way. A romance should have you crying into a box of tissues, get you hot under the collar, make you laugh and leave your heart feeling full. After all, life is better when there’s love in the air. This list contains some of the most romantic ,bittersweet and heart-wrenching movies which I believe everyone should watch atleast once in their lifetime!

39. Rab ne bana di jodi

Director: Aditya Chopra

Cast: Shah Rukh Khan, Anushka Sharma

Surinder, a simple man, falls for a vivacious Tani and gets married to her. In order to impress her, he undergoes a complete makeover and becomes Raj.

7.2/10

38. Before We Go

Director: Chris Evans

Cast: Chris Evans, Alice Eve

Nick, a street musician, meets Brooke in the subway when she misses her last train home. As he tries to take her to her destination, the two confide in each other and confront their long-held fears.

6.8/10  

Romance Drama Watch Now

37. About Time

Director: Richard Curtis

Cast: Rachel McAdams, Domhnall Gleeson

Like all the men in his family, Tim Lake possesses the power to travel in time. With the advice of his father, he uses his special ability to pursue his romantic interest, Mary.

7.8/10  

Romance Fantasy Watch Now

36. True Romance

Director: Tony Scott

Cast: Patricia Arquette, Christian Slater, Gary Oldman

A comic-book nerd and Elvis fanatic Clarence (Christian Slater) and a prostitute named Alabama (Patricia Arquette) fall in love. Clarence breaks the news to her pimp and ends up killing him. He grabs a suitcase of cocaine on his way out thinking it is Alabama's clothing. The two hit the road for California hoping to sell the cocaine, but the mob is soon after them.

7.9/10  

Romance Crime Watch Now

35. The Fault in our stars

Director: Josh Boone

Cast: Shailene Woodley, Hansel, Ansel Elgort

Two cancer-afflicted teenagers Hazel and Augustus meet at a cancer support group. The two of them embark on a journey to visit a reclusive author in Amsterdam.
